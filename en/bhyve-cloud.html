bhyve.cloud

You can host resources on our experimental clouds bhyve.cloud and bitclouds.sh, depending on payment method you prefer ( RUR/Visa, Paypal, Bitcoin ), with customizable resources and automation capabilities (API). Services offer virtual machines on bhyve hypervisor. Using our services, you can easily explore capabilities of hypervisor, which one is open-source and distributed under free BSD license.

Using these resources, you'll support CBSD Project. Moreover, using bhyve.cloud and bitclouds.sh and sharing info about them, you help CBSD project by spreading technology and extending rapidly growing community. Project income covers infrastructure expenses and helps improve the quality of the service.
